term: AI Agent

  An autonomous AI system that, given a goal, plans actions, executes them,
  evaluates results, and iterates to solve problems without direct human intervention.
examples:
  - "An AI agent automatically categorizes and responds to customer complaints."
  - "Agents like ReAct and AutoGPT use tools to handle complex multi-step tasks."
