---
term: Eok-kka
definition: |
  Online fandom that criticizes and ridicules celebrity behavior on SNS.
  Etymology possibly blends "억울한" (aggrieved) with "audience" (청중).
examples:
  - "When a celebrity causes controversy, eok-kka swarms immediately."
  - "Excessive eok-kka can cross into cyberbullying."
reading: /ʌk-kɑ/
aliases: [aggrieved audience, anti-fan]
origin: Emerged from online fandom culture in the late 2010s.
---

Reveals the double-edged nature of online fandom.
Critical debate centers on where legitimate criticism becomes harassment.
