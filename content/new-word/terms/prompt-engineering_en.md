---
term: Prompt Engineering
definition: |
  Systematically designing and refining input queries (prompts)
  to maximize the performance and accuracy of Large Language Models.
examples:
  - "Good prompt engineering improved ChatGPT output quality by 3x."
  - "Adding few-shot examples in the prompt makes the model more accurate."
reading: /prɑːmpt ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/
aliases: [prompt design, prompting]
origin: Became critical as LLMs commercialized starting in 2022.
---

Essential skill in the era of generative AI.
The same model produces completely different results based on prompt quality.
