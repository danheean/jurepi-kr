---
term: King-Annoyed
definition: |
  Slang meaning "angry like a king," or "seriously frustrated."
  Expresses extreme frustration or annoyance, amplified from casual anger.
examples:
  - "Did you see that video? I'm king-annoyed. How could that happen..."
  - "The late bus made me king-annoyed and I ended up late anyway."
reading: /kɪŋ.bɑt.dɑ/
aliases: [infuriated, livid, super annoyed]
origin: Popularized in online communities from late 2010s.
---

Modern intensified slang for expressing emotion.
Conveys deeper anger than simply "very angry."
