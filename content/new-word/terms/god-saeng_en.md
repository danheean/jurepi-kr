---
term: God Life
definition: |
  A Korean slang combining "god" (신) with "life/living" (생).
  Describes a perfectly organized, productive, and admirable lifestyle.
examples:
  - "I've decided to live a god life, so I exercise and read every day."
  - "Waking up early, meditating, and growing yourself—that's a god life."
reading: /ɡɑːd laɪf/
aliases: [divine life, god-like living]
origin: Spread through Instagram, YouTube, and SNS around 2019.
---

Reflects MZ generation's idealization of self-discipline and growth.
Aspirational lifestyle philosophy rooted in personal development.
