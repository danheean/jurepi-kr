---
term: Yareu
definition: |
  A Korean interjection shouted when you're excited or thrilled,
  roughly equivalent to "yay!", "woo-hoo!", or "yes!".
  It caught on among Korean Gen Z (teens and twenties).
examples:
  - "Exams are finally over — yareu!"
  - "Chicken for dinner tonight? Yareu!"
reading: /ja.ɾɯ/
aliases: [yareu~, yar]
origin: |
  Its exact origin is debated and unconfirmed (theories include a shortening
  of the Japanese "yaruna," and spread through YouTubers and comedians on air).
  It spread rapidly among Korean Gen Z via short-form video around 2025.
---

A short, upbeat exclamation of joy or excitement.
Typically used as a reaction or under-the-breath cheer when something good happens.
