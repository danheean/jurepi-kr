---
term: Plot-Twist Joy
definition: |
  Compressed from "spoiler burns joy."
  Describes enjoying stories like dramas, movies, web novels even after knowing the ending.
examples:
  - "That drama is plot-twist joy—the ending is spoiled but still worth watching."
  - "Web novels are often plot-twist joy due to complex storytelling."
reading: /sbul.dʒɛ/
aliases: [spoiler-proof, ending-rich]
origin: Popular in online communities since early 2020s.
---

Reflects how content consumption habits are evolving.
Shows that "how" matters as much as "what" in storytelling.
